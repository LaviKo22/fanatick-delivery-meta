Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Fanatick operates a ticket delivery automation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • WhatsApp phone numbers for delivery communications
  • Names provided for delivery identification
  • Ticket and seat information related to deliveries
  • Screenshots sent during the delivery process
  • Message history between our bot and clients

How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate ticket delivery via WhatsApp
  • Verify wallet additions and removals
  • Maintain delivery records and proof of delivery

WhatsApp Communications

Our service uses the WhatsApp Business API to communicate with clients regarding ticket deliveries. By engaging with our WhatsApp bot, you consent to receiving automated messages related to your ticket delivery.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. Delivery records are retained for 90 days after completion.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide our services.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP
